ATS Optimization Tips

Use Standard Headings

"Work Experience", "Education", "Skills" are ATS-friendly. Avoid creative titles.

Include Relevant Keywords

Mirror language from the job description. ATS scans for matching terms.

Avoid Graphics & Tables

Most ATS can't read text in headers, footers, or tables. Keep it simple.

Use Standard Fonts

Stick to Arial, Times New Roman, Calibri, or Helvetica for best compatibility.

Don't Use Columns

Multi-column layouts often get scrambled in ATS parsing.

Quantify Achievements

Use numbers and metrics ("Increased sales by 30%") for better impact.
